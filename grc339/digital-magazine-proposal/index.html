Introduction

One Bag Travels

One Bag Travels is a magazine that shares guides, tips, and stories for minimal backpacking for budget-friendly trips. Our target audience are young travels who want to see the world without emptying their wallet. Articles will consist from iternaries for certain destinations, accommodation recommendations, gear suggestions, to money tips. Whether they have taken many trips around the world, to a novice that wants to travel affordabily, we have information that will be useful to anyone.
